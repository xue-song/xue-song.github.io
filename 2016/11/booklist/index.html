我的经济学书架

Veröffentlicht am 2016-11-07

一些笔者参考过并推荐的经济学教科书：

Mathematics for Economics:

  • Simon and Blume (1994) Mathematics for Economists. Amazon

  • Wainwright and Chiang (2004) Fundamental Methods of Mathematical Economics. Amazon

Econometrics:

  • Green (2011) Econometrics Analysis. Amazon

  • Fumio Hayashi (2000) Econometrics. Amazon

  • Jeff Wooldridge (2010) Econometric Analysis of Cross Section and Panel Data. Amazon

Microeconomics:

  • Mas-Colell, Whinston, Green (1995) Microeconomics Theory. Amazon

  • Hal Varian (1992) Microeconomic Analysis. Amazon

  • Ariel Rubinstein (2014) Lecture Notes in Microeconomics Theory. Free E-book

Macroeconomics:

  • David Romer (2011) Advanced Macroeconomics. Amazon

  • Robert Barro (2003) Economic Growth. 2nd ed. Amazon

Game theory:

  • Joseph E. Harrington (2008) Games, Strategies and Decision Making. Amazon

  • Fudenberg and Tirole (1991) Game Theory. Amazon

Agency theory and information economics:

  • Laffont and Martimort (2002) The Theory of Incentives: The Principal-Agent Model Amazon

  • Bolton and Dewatripont (2004) Contract Theory. Amazon

  • Macho-Stadler, Perez-Castrillo, and Watt (2001) An Introduction to the Economics of Information: Incentives and Contracts. Amazon

Corporate Finance:

  • J Tirole (2006) The Theory of Corporate Finance. Amazon

Asset Pricing:

Cochrane (2005) Asset Pricing. Amazon

George Pennacchi (2007) Theory of Asset Pricing. Amazon

Costis Skiadas (2009) Asset Pricing Theory. Amazon

General economics:

  • Microeconomics:

    • J. McMillan (2003) Reinventing the Bazaar: A Natural History of Markets. Amazon

  • Game theory:

    • A.K. Dixit (2010) The Art of Strategy: A Game Theorist’s Guide to Success in Business and Life. Amazon

  • Macroeconomics:

    • Robert J. Barro (1997) Getting It Right: Markets and Choices in a Free Society. Amazon

  • Finance:

    • B.G. Malkiel (2011) A Random Walk Down Wall Street. 10th ed. Amazon

  • Behavioral:

    • Richard H. Thaler (2016) Misbehaving: The Making of Behavioral Economics. Amazon

    • Dan Ariely (2010) Predictably Irrational: The Hidden Forces That Shape Our Decisions. Amazon

  • To Be Completed.
